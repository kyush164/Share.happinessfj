About Us

At Share.happinessfj, we create unique crafts, cards, and gifts designed to bring a smile to your face. We use mostly recycled and commonly found materials to ensure our products are eco-friendly and sustainable.

Our creations include homemade bouquets, cards and crafts, and decorations and designs. We take pride in making each piece with love and attention to detail.
